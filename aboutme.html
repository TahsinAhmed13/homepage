About Me

Dear Internet,
    I am a programmer who has grown tired of using his mouse. And so I'm writing to you about my noble cause. Along with a small group of programmers called the 'development team', we have joined together in an effort to abolish the right to use a mouse on one's computer. We have started by creating a mouse-free website that uses only a terminal to navigate. By pressing T you can open up the terminal. Go to ~/learn.html to learn more.
